About Us

Welcome To
Unlimited Financial Services

The founder of UFS , Mr. Alex Mpharoane together with his wife Tholang Qwendoline Mpharoane established Unlimited Financial Services LTD in 2017 in Lesotho as a public company under the Companies Act 2011 to serve the employees of government with suitable solutions for the type of environment they work in. The two shareholders hold 100% ownership of the company. UFS has an authorized capital of M1m and issued and paid up capital of M200K.

UFS is currently operating in 6 districts namely , Maseru where it is head quartered, mafeteng , Berea , leribe , Bothabothe and mokhotlong.

Our Vision

To be the preferred provider of customer centric financial solutions to employees in South Africa.

Our Mission

To empower the economically active but marginalized workers by providing relevant, timely and effective financial solutions through skilled and motivated workforce with the latest technology appropriate for the financial sector.
